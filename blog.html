Day 1 - Tokyo

Published on 08-02-2021



Today, I arrived in Tokyo, Japan! I have been to Japan for many years and it will always be my favourite country. I woke up super early and caught an uber to the airport. I had a direct flight from Gold Coast Airport to Tokyo. I arrived there just after 6pm. It was a long flight and I usually don't like long flights. I was really excited when I arrived in Japan. The first thing I always do when I arrive is to buy onigiri, which is like a rice ball filled with delicious fillings such as tuna and salmon roe.


Day 2 - Shibuya

Published on 08-03-2021


Today is the second day of my trip in Japan. Shibuya has always been one of my favourite places to visit in Japan. It is known for the cross-intersection and the hachiko dog statue. There are quite a lot of things to do in Shibuya but it is popular for shopping and the department stores. Today, I will be shopping for some clothes and eating some delicious food!


Day 3 - Akihabara

Published on 09-03-2021


Today is my third day of my Japan trip. I feel like time is going by so fast! I can't believe its my third day. Akihabara is a place I must always visit when I visit Japan. Even when I lived in Japan, I used to visit it at least once in a month. It is a place I love for its vibrant colours and the game crane machines. If you are an avid fan of anime and manga, it is a place you must visit. Akihabara is filled with lots of electronic shops and sells anime goods, figures, manga and for the adventorous; maid cafes, which I have been to twice. It is an interesting experience.

Day 4 - Harajuku

Published on 10-03-2021


Harajuku! A place which is known for its fashion. Whenever you go there, you will always find people dressed in vibrant colours and also displaying their own personality through their clothes.

Day 5 - Ayase

Published on 11-03-2021


In 2017, I had the oppurtunity to study in Japan for a year at Akamonkai Japanese Language School. I lived in a place called Ayase, which is just a 40 minute train ride from Shinjuku. So everytime I go back to Japan, I always visit Ayase. It is a place that will always hold dear to my heart and is like a second home to me . I would always visit the restaurants I visited during my time living there. I made good friends with one of the restaurant owners and I always visit him when I go to Japan.
